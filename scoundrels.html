Scoundrels of the Atlantic

The Process

The Museum of Natural Sciences in North Carolina is wishing to increase interest in state history and exploring the museum. They requested a separate responsive website based off an exhibit, current or previous, that would be interactive and lead back to the main museum website.

Challenges

This came with quite a few challenges. First is to design a responsive interactive site that would reach out to younger demographics. Second is to generate interest in learning more and possible nudging that curiosity towards the museum. On top of that this was a project that involved working as a team.

How to make it successful?

Brainstorming as a team we came to the conclusion that by creating a few important set pieces that require user interactive would engage the curiosity of the user. After sparking the users' curosity, we led them to prominent link back page to the museum in order for them to explore further. To boost this we would need to successfully reach out to younger on the go generations thus the site required responsive capabilities from desktop to mobile.
