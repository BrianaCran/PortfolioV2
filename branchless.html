Design from the Ground up

The Process

A hypothetical upcoming banking company called Branchless launched in early 2016. They brought change to traditional banking by having no physical branch locations. As a result, it was decided that a banking app was crucial in order to continue growing their customer base as well as supporting their existing customers better. For this project I had to create the branding or style guide lines as there were none set out. After developing a logo and choosing a good scheme of colors I jumped into brainstorming what actions user would like or need. After getting a few ideas of necessary needs I began sketching how these interactions would look like. After sketching was completed the full color mockups started and the mockups were turned into a prototype in Invision. This project was not brought into the development phase.
